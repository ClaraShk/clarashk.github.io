About

I am currently the Head of Research at ChainCode Labs, where I lead research initiatives in blockchain technology and cryptoeconomics. Previously, I was at the Simons Institute for the Theory of Computing at Berkeley and at CalTech.

I graduated from Tel Aviv University with a PhD in Mathematics under the supervision of Prof. Noga Alon. My thesis was titled 'Generalized Turán Type Problems and other Questions'. I completed my master's degree at the Weizmann Institute under Prof. Ehud Friedgut, with a thesis titled Thresholds of monotone properties with small minterms.
